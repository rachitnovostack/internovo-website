How does IVPL use cookies and web beacons?

Cookies and web beacons are technologies that IVPL and our third-party service providers use to collect Web Browser Information in order to track how the IVPL Sites and IVPL Services are used. Cookies are small data files that are stored on Your computer’s hard drive and web beacons are tiny graphics embedded on web pages. These technologies allow us to: provide You with a personalized experience by recognizing You when You return to the IVPL Sites; better understand our users by tracking how they use the IVPL Services; improve the design and content of the IVPL Sites by tracking how users interact with the IVPL Sites; and identify and prevent fraudulent activity. We may link Web Browser Information to any personal information that You submit while on the IVPL Sites. Our third-party service providers may use cookies and web beacons to provide analytics services to IVPL, or to serve You advertisements in connection with the IVPL Services.
