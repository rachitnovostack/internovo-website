Internovo Ventures

Internovo Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is a JV between Interface Ventures and Novostack.

An Ideal combination of fin & tech, Interface Ventures is an investments, incubation & advisory platform and Novostack is a new-age technology company.

Our mission is to help people understand finance and make smart financial decision through our fintech platforms.

Meet Our Founders

Karan Desai
