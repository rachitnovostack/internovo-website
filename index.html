Meet Our Founders

Karan Desai

Karan brings a rich mix of hard operations, investing and fintech deployment experience to Internovo™ Ventures. He has over a decade of financial services experience across firms like PwC, Centrum Capital, etc. before he built an MSME focused NBFC TruCap Finance Ltd. which is also listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange.He is a distinction student from the University of Westminster, London (Masters) and Narsee Monjee College, Mumbai (Bachelors). Karan is a Non-Executive Director of the board of RULoans Distribution Service Pvt. Ltd., which is one of India's leading retail loans & credit card distribution companies. With a rich legacy of over 2 decades, the company distributes over US$ 2 billion of the retail loans across India on an annual basis.
